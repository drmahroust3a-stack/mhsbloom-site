MHS BLOOM — Neutral Shelf

Browser extension · Privacy Policy · Last updated 17 July 2026

The short version: the extension collects nothing about you and sends no personal data anywhere. All matching happens on your own device.

What the extension does

When you open a product page on a supported store, the extension reads the product's title text from that page and matches it — locally, on your device — against MHS BLOOM's public, neutral reference of dermacosmetic products. If it finds a match, it shows a small panel with the neutral facts we hold on that product.

Data we collect

None.

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your identity, browsing history, or the pages you visit.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, cookies, fingerprinting, or advertising.
  • We do not have any server that receives your activity.

What stays on your device

  • A cached copy of our public product reference (the same open dataset published at mhsbloom.com/data). It is downloaded once and refreshed periodically so matching is fast and works offline. It contains no personal data.
  • Any product you choose to "request" (via the request button) is saved only in your browser's local storage. It is not sent anywhere in this version.

Network requests

The only network request the extension makes is to https://mhsbloom.com to download the public product reference. It contacts no other server and sends no data with that request beyond a standard file download.

Permissions, and why

  • Storage — to cache the public reference on your device so it loads instantly.
  • Access to supported store pages — to read the product title and show the panel there. The extension only reads the visible product name; it does not read your account, forms, passwords, or payment details.

Neutrality

MHS BLOOM is independent and payment-blind: no brand can pay to change a result or its ranking. The extension shows the same neutral facts to everyone.

Children

The extension is a general skincare reference and is not directed at children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
