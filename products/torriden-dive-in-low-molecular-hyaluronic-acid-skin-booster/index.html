DIVE-IN Low Molecular Hyaluronic Acid Skin Booster

DIVE-IN Low Molecular Hyaluronic Acid Skin Booster is a serum by Torriden®, part of the DIVE-IN range. It has a liquid texture and is fragrance-free, in a 50ml size.

KEY INGREDIENTS
  • Hyaluronic AcidA humectant that draws and holds water in the upper skin layers, leaving skin feeling plumper and more hydrated.
  • PanthenolA water-attracting, barrier-soothing ingredient that helps skin hold moisture and feel calmer and smoother..
  • AllantoinA gentle soothing and skin-conditioning ingredient that helps calm and protect the skin's surface and supports a smoother, softer feel..
BEST SUITED TO

All skin types, especially dehydrated skin wanting a watery first step.
