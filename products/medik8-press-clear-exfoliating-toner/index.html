Press & Clear Exfoliating Toner

Press & Clear Exfoliating Toner is a toner by Medik8®, part of the Press & Clear range. It has a liquid texture and is fragrance-free, in a 200ml size.

Key ingredients

  • Salicylic Acid (BHA)An oil-soluble exfoliant that works inside pores to lift away dead surface cells and excess oil, supporting the look of clearer, smoother skin and a more refined appearance on oily skin (the look of fewer blackheads and whiteheads)..
  • Mandelic Acid
  • NiacinamideA water-soluble form of vitamin B3 that supports the look of a healthy skin barrier, helps the appearance of even tone, and calms the look of redness..

Best suited to

Oily, combination and blemish-prone skin.
