Heliocare 360° Water Gel SPF50+

Heliocare 360° Water Gel SPF50+ is a sunscreen by Heliocare®, part of the 360° range. It has a gel texture and is fragrance-free, in a 50ml size.

Key ingredients

  • Fernblock
  • Vitamin CA water-soluble antioxidant that helps brighten uneven tone, supports the look of firmness by aiding the skin's collagen-making process, and adds daytime protection alongside sunscreen..
  • Vitamin EA fat-soluble antioxidant that supports the look of the skin's lipid barrier and helps neutralize free radicals, helping skin look healthier and more even, especially after sun exposure..

Best suited to

All skin types, especially normal and dehydrated skin.
