Clarifying Cleanser

Clarifying Cleanser is a cleanser by Murad®, part of the Clarifying range. It has a gel texture and is lightly scented, in a 200ml size.

Key ingredients

  • Salicylic AcidAn oil-soluble exfoliant that works inside pores to lift away dead surface cells and excess oil, supporting the look of clearer, smoother skin and a more refined appearance on oily skin (the look of fewer blackheads and whiteheads)..
  • Green Tea Extract
