What is MHS BLOOM?

MHS BLOOM is an independent dermacosmetics reference app — 1,682 products from 82 pharmacy-channel skincare brands, each reviewed with sources. Its newest feature, BLOOM Scan, lets anyone photograph the front of a product and instantly learn whether it fits their skin — with better-fit alternatives at every price.

  • Independent by design: no brand can pay to appear; verdicts and rankings are payment-blind. No ads, no affiliate links.
  • 1,682 products · 82 brands — every claim traceable to a source.
  • BLOOM Scan: photograph → identify → "right for your skin?" → alternatives at every price.
  • Fully bilingual: English and Arabic with complete right-to-left design.
  • Privacy-first: no tracking; the skin profile stays on the user's device; scan photos are processed instantly and never stored.
  • Free to browse; BLOOM PRO ($39.99/year) unlocks the personal decision layer.
  • Built in Riyadh by a small independent team, for a global audience. Live worldwide on iOS and Android.
